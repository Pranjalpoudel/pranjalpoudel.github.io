About Me

I am an Electronic Communication and Information Engineer who loves bridging the gap between hardware and web software. With a strong academic foundation (Full Scholarship at NCE) and a consistent record of academic excellence (Semester Topper), I thrive on solving complex problems.

My passion lies in innovation—whether it's designing intricate circuits, coding efficient algorithms, or expressing creativity through painting and cooking. I am always eager to learn, build, and contribute to impactful technology.

My technical expertise spans from Embedded Systems and IoT to modern Web Development. Whether it's designing Circuit Boards (PCB) or building interactive React applications, I specialize in creating full-stack solutions where hardware meets the web.

My Vision & Approach: I believe that the future of technology lies in the seamless integration of physical hardware and cloud-based intelligence. My approach focuses on scalability, performance, and user-centric design. By mastering both ends of the spectrum—from low-level C programming on microcontrollers to high-level JavaScript frameworks—I am able to build cohesive systems that are both efficient and impactful.
